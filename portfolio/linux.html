Neon Style Wallpapers


Some of the viewers of my YouTube channel requested that I make some wallpapers for linux. Specifically this style of wallpaper was requested, after I made a video about how I designed a thumbnail in a similar style.

Just in case you were every thinking of trying to do something similar: don't. I am a linux user full-time, so I knew there were about a million different linux distributions. But I thought it would be easy enough to just download nerd fonts, and use all the linux distros there. I wish it was that easy. But about three days of masking out logos, and creating mattes, I finally got to a point where people seemed to be happy.
